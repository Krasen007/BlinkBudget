## üéØ Key Competitors Overview

### 1. **Mint** (Intuit)

- **Strengths**: Established brand, bank integration, comprehensive features
- **Weaknesses**: Complex interface, US-only, expensive, data privacy concerns
- **Pricing**: Free with ads, Premium ~$4.99/month
- **Entry Time**: 8-12 clicks per expense

### 2. **YNAB** (You Need A Budget)

- **Strengths**: Strong budgeting methodology, educational content
- **Weaknesses**: Steep learning curve, expensive, time-intensive setup
- **Pricing**: 14-day free trial, then $14.99/month
- **Entry Time**: 6-10 clicks per expense

### 3. **PocketGuard**

- **Strengths**: Simple interface, good mobile app
- **Weaknesses**: Limited features, subscription-based, slow performance
- **Pricing**: Free basic, Premium $3.99/month
- **Entry Time**: 5-8 clicks per expense

### 4. **Spendee**

- **Strengths**: Good design, shared wallets, decent features
- **Weaknesses**: Can be slow, limited free version, complex for quick entry
- **Pricing**: Free with limitations, Premium $4.99/month
- **Entry Time**: 4-7 clicks per expense

### 5. **Wallet by BudgetBakers**

- **Strengths**: Clean interface, good visualizations
- **Weaknesses**: Limited platforms, subscription required for most features
- **Pricing**: 7-day trial, then $5.99/month
- **Entry Time**: 5-9 clicks per expense
