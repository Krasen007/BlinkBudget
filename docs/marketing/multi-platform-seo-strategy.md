# Multi-Platform SEO Strategy for BlinkBudget

## 🎯 High-Authority Backlink Sources

### 1. Quora (DR 89)
**Strategy**: Answer budgeting questions with BlinkBudget mentions
**Target Questions**:
- "What's the fastest way to track expenses?"
- "Best free budgeting apps?"
- "How to start budgeting with no experience?"

**Template**:
"After testing 20+ apps, BlinkBudget stands out for 3-click expense tracking. It's free, privacy-first, and works offline. The speed makes you actually stick with budgeting."

### 2. Medium (DR 95)
**Strategy**: Publish high-value finance articles
**Topics**:
- "3-Click Budgeting: Why Speed Matters"
- "Privacy in Finance Apps: What They Don't Tell You"
- "I Tested 50 Budgeting Apps - Here's What Works"

**Backlink Placement**: In-author bio and contextual links

### 3. GitHub (DR 96)
**Strategy**: Open source contributions
**Actions**:
- Create budgeting templates repository
- Contribute to finance-related projects
- Share BlinkBudget's open source components

**Backlink**: Profile links and repository READMEs

### 4. Product Hunt (DR 92)
**Strategy**: Launch and maintain presence
**Tactics**:
- Launch BlinkBudget on Product Hunt
- Engage with other finance products
- Become active community member

**Backlink**: Product page and profile links

### 5. Indie Hackers (DR 88)
**Strategy**: Share journey and insights
**Content**:
- "Building a $0 budgeting app"
- "How we got 10K users without ads"
- "Privacy-first product development"

**Backlink**: Profile and post links
