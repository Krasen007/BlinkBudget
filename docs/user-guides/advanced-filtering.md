## 4. Applying and Clearing Filters

- **Real-time Updates**: On most devices, filters update in real-time as you type or select options.
- **Apply Filters**: Click the primary "Apply Filters" button at the bottom of the panel to ensure all changes are committed.
- **Clear All**: Use the "Clear All" button in the top right of the panel to instantly reset all filters to their default state.
