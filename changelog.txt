# Changelog v1.31

- **Core Analytics & UX Refinement**: 
  - Restructured the "Time of Day Patterns" dashboard into easier-to-read week-based rows with compact day visualizations.
  - Implemented an advanced Anomaly Detection and Optimization Engine with Bulgarian and English language support.
  - Optimized data processing to use local timezones, resolving errors where spending was shifted into the wrong day.

- **Spending Patterns & Insights**:
  - Improved the "Category Trends" chart to prioritize your top 6 spending categories while keeping the rest easily accessible.
  - Enhanced the overall "Spending Baseline" analysis with more accurate historical charts and benchmarking calculations.
  - Refined reports to exclude the current (incomplete) month, providing a clearer look at your past spending trends.

- **Security & Stability**:
  - Significantly hardened application security by implementing advanced XSS protection and stricter URL validation within the image loading service.
  - Resolved multiple calculation errors in benchmarking and anomaly details.
  - Streamlined the application by removing the defunct "Scenarios" and "Spending Predictions" features for a faster, cleaner experience.
