### BlinkBudget v1.16.0 Changelog

* **Haptic Feedback Removal**: Completely removed haptic feedback (vibrations) across the entire application for a more consistent and power-efficient experience.
* **Backup & Restore**: Introduced a comprehensive backup and restore system, allowing users to safely manage and sync their financial data.
* **Enhanced Transaction Entry**: Improved the 'Add Transaction' experience by maintaining field focus when switching transaction types (Expense/Income/Transfer/Refund).
* **UI & Style Refinements**: 
    - Standardized transfer styling across all account types.
    - Improved the Reports View header for better visibility and layout on mobile devices.
* **Performance & Security**:
    - Optimized bundle sizing and application loading times for a Snappier feel.
    - Implemented critical security updates via Snyk.
* **General**: Updated internal dependencies and marketing materials.
