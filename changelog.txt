- **Tags System** - Organize transactions with custom tags accessible from Settings → Categories for better organization and filtering
- **Enhanced Mobile UX** - Added mobile prompts for financial goals to improve goal setup experience on mobile devices
- **Budget Navigation** - Clicking budget summary card in Reports now takes you directly to the Budgets section in Financial Planning
